About Us

ShakesBierre, a fine dining restaurant that offers you a fusion of French and Indian cuisine. Our name is inspired by the adventurous spirit of our chef and owner, who loves to explore new flavors and create dishes that surprise and delight our guests. ShakesBierre was founded in 2024, with the vision of bringing a fresh and exciting dining experience to Indiranagar, one of the most vibrant and cosmopolitan areas in Bangalore. Our menu features a variety of dishes, from classic French delicacies like escargot and ratatouille, to innovative Indian specialties like tandoori chicken and biryani. We use only the finest ingredients, sourced locally and ethically, and prepare them with care and creativity. At ShakesBierre, we value our guests and strive to provide them with exceptional service and quality. Our staff are friendly, attentive, and knowledgeable, and will make you feel at home in our cozy and elegant ambiance. We also offer catering services for events, parties, and corporate functions, as well as online ordering and delivery for your convenience. We invite you to join us at Reaver, where you will discover a world of flavors and a dining experience like no other. Book your table with us today and get ready to be amazed by our culinary creations.
